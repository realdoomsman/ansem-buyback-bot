How It Works

Every step is automated, verifiable on-chain, and protected against MEV extraction.

1
👁️

Monitor Fees

The bot watches the designated fee wallet 24/7 via WebSocket. When Pump.fun creator fees arrive as SOL, it detects them instantly.

2
🔄

Buy-Back $ANSEM

The SOL is swapped for $ANSEM tokens via Jupiter Aggregator, finding the best price across all Solana DEXes. Submitted privately through Jito bundles.

3
🎁

Airdrop to Holders

Purchased tokens are distributed pro-rata to all $ANSEM holders. Hold 10% of supply? You get 10% of the airdrop. Simple.

Security

3-Layer MEV Protection

Every buy-back is shielded from sandwich attacks and front-running bots.

🛡️ Layer 1

Jito Private Bundles

Swap transactions are submitted directly to Jito validators as private bundles. They never appear in the public mempool where MEV bots watch. The swap is invisible until it's already confirmed on-chain.

⚡ Layer 2

Strict Slippage Control

Every swap has a maximum slippage tolerance of 0.5%. If the price moves more than this between the quote and execution, the transaction automatically reverts — limiting any potential damage.

📊 Layer 3

Price Impact Check

Before executing, the bot validates Jupiter's price impact report. If the swap would move the price more than 5%, it's rejected. This prevents buying during extremely illiquid conditions.
